💡 Tips for Freshers

📄
Build a Strong Resume
Use our free Resume Builder to create an ATS-friendly resume that gets noticed by recruiters.
🎯
Apply to Internships First
Internships give you real experience. Even 3 months of internship can boost your chances greatly.
🔗
Build LinkedIn Profile
70% of recruiters use LinkedIn. Create a complete profile with your skills, projects and education.
📚
Get Certified
Free certifications from Google, Coursera, NPTEL add value to your resume and show initiative.
💻
Build Projects
A GitHub with 2-3 projects shows employers your skills better than marks alone.
📧
Follow Up
After applying, follow up with a polite email after 3-5 days. It shows enthusiasm and initiative.
